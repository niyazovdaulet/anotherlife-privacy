Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 December 2025

AnotherLife is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the AnotherLife mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User-Provided Information

When using AnotherLife, you may choose to provide:

  • Habit titles, descriptions, and notes
  • Challenge participation data
  • Nickname, avatar, and profile information

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:

  • App usage data (e.g., streaks, progress)
  • Device type and operating system
  • Anonymous analytics to improve the app

1.3 Cloud Sync via Firebase

The app uses Firebase for secure authentication, data storage, and syncing. Firebase may collect:

  • Authentication tokens
  • User-generated content (habits, challenges)
  • Error logs

All data stored in Firebase is protected using encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Sync your habits and progress across devices
  • Provide challenge features and leaderboards
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Maintain secure access to your account

3. How We Protect Your Data

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted network communication (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure Firebase authentication
  • Limited access to stored user data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.

4. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

AnotherLife uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication – secure login
  • Firebase Firestore – cloud data storage
  • Firebase Crashlytics – crash reporting
  • Firebase Analytics (optional)

Please refer to Google’s privacy policy for more details: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Children’s Privacy

AnotherLife is not intended for users under 4 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region (e.g., EU or California), you may have rights to:

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Access the data we store
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Delete your account

To exercise these rights, contact us using the email below.

Data Export

You may request or export a copy of your personal data stored in the app. This may include information such as habits, completion history, notes, and related metadata. Data exports are generated locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Premium Features

AnotherLife offers an optional Premium membership that unlocks additional features. Purchasing Premium is optional and does not affect access to the core functionality of the app. All subscriptions and payments are processed and managed through Apple’s App Store.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.
